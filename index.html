Privacy Policy for Snippy

Last Updated: July 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Snippy for Quickbase ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our Chrome extension (the "Service"). Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

Information We Do NOT Collect or Store

We want to be crystal clear about what we don't do. Snippy is designed to be a client-side tool that enhances your Quickbase experience without compromising your data.

  • Your Code and Formulas: Snippy does NOT save, store, transmit, or log any of the code or formulas you write. Your work is processed in your browser and sent directly back to your Quickbase page or, with your explicit action, to your own Google Drive account. It never touches our servers.
  • Your Quickbase Data: Snippy does not read, access, or store any of the data within your Quickbase applications or tables.

Information We Collect and How We Use It

We collect the absolute minimum information necessary for the extension to function and to verify donor support.

1. Google Drive Integration (Optional)

If you choose to use the "Save to Google Drive" feature, you will be asked to grant the extension permission to access your Google Drive.

  • Permission Requested: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file
  • What this allows: This is a restricted permission that allows Snippy to create a new folder named "Snippy for Quickbase" in your Google Drive and to create, read, and update files **only within that specific folder**.
  • What this does NOT allow: The extension **cannot** see, read, or access any other files or folders in your Google Drive. Its access is strictly limited to the folder it creates for its own use.

2. Donation Verification (Optional)

If you choose to support Snippy through a donation, we need a way to verify your status to unlock donor-only features.

  • Information Collected: We will store the email address you use during the donation process.
  • How it's used: This email address is used solely to verify your donation status when you enter it into the extension. It will not be used for marketing, sold, or shared with any third parties.

3. Feedback and Suggestions (Optional, Donors-Only)

If you are a verified donor and choose to submit feedback or feature suggestions through the extension:

  • Information Collected: We collect the content of your message.
  • How it's used: This information is used exclusively to improve the Snippy extension.

Data Security

All data transmitted between the extension and our verification services (for donations) is encrypted using industry-standard TLS (HTTPS). We take reasonable measures to protect the minimal information we store.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@snippyforquickbase.com.
