There’s no dev team. No VC funding. Just one person, one browser, and a deep personal vendetta against bad indentation.

Gene Bixler

Quickbase Developer since 2013 and has worked for Quickbase, Quickbase partners, and Quickbase customers — which is basically like seeing the Matrix from all three angles. Built mission-critical apps, untangled legacy horrors, and survived more formula fields than any human should.

Gene created Snippy because Quickbase's editor was giving him rage-induced carpal tunnel. When he’s not building clean interfaces or fixing problems Quickbase never will, he’s probably talking to an AI, reformatting other people’s code, or quietly judging nested If() statements.
